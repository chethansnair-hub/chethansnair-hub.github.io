Hello there! I am a 13-year-old boy who loves to code and learn new things! My coding journey started 5 years ago at the age of 8 when I discovered Scratch.

I fell in love with the platform. Seeing that I spent more time on Scratch than sleeping, my parents enrolled me in a class teaching Python. My love for coding only grew and turned into a passion. From Python, I discovered Roblox Studio and learned its language, Lua. With the help of a relative, I learned Java. Around this time, I upgraded my old 2020 computer. Taking advantage of its power, I started to use Unity for Reinforcement Learning and learned C#.

Another one of my passions is volunteering. Mahatma Gandhi once said, "You must be the change you wish to see in the world." From a young age, I have always wanted to give back to the community. At 10 years old, I joined my school's leadership program. Last year, I joined my city's youth council.In October, an elementary student was almost hit by a car while crossing the road that separated our community from the nearby school. In order to solve this, alongside other worried community members, I started a petition to install a stop sign where students would cross. Using my position in the council, I managed to bring the Mayor's attention to this matter. Because of this, the petition was quickly passed and a stop sign is currently being planned for installation.

Volunteering and coding have always been equally important to me. Because of this, I thought, "why not do both!" To accomplish this, I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
